How does this work?

The new user interface provides simple, one-stop searching for books and e-books, videos, articles, digital media, and more. Please use your WesternAccount to sign-in to:

  • Renew books and videos
  • Create favourites lists
  • View full search results. (Some databases only show results when you're signed in.)

You may notice changes as the new interface is improved and finalised.

Where can I get help?

Chat with our Online Librarian or ask campus library staff.
